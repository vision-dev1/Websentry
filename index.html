Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our scanning technology and plans.

Is the scan safe for my website? keyboard_arrow_down

Yes, absolutely. WebSentry performs passive and non-intrusive active scanning. We simulate traffic patterns that do not disrupt your service or modify your data. It is safe for production environments.

Do I need to install any software? keyboard_arrow_down

No. WebSentry is a 100% cloud-based SaaS platform. You can start scanning simply by entering your URL. For internal networks, we offer a Docker container agent, but it is optional.

How long does a scan take? keyboard_arrow_down

Quick scans typically finish in under 60 seconds. Full, deep-dive vulnerability assessments can take anywhere from 10 to 45 minutes depending on the size and complexity of your application.

What happens if vulnerabilities are found? keyboard_arrow_down

We provide a detailed report categorized by severity (Critical, High, Medium, Low). Each finding includes remediation steps, code snippets, and references to help your developers fix the issue fast.
